Realistic Artificial Daylight Screen

The Realistic Artificial Daylight Screen is a screen that uses the light board from a broken tv or monitor that produces light that looks very similar to daylight. This screen can be used in the middle of the night which it also can improve productivity and it's great for taking pictures. This is another project made by DIY Perks.

The Smart Mirror is a smart mirror that doesn't steam up with the use of water circulating behind the front screen of the mirror. It can be customized to display whatever information is wanted and can play music and videos. Another project by DIY Perks

Vacuum Chamber

The Vacuum Chamber is something that can be used to test items inside the chamber. The chamber was made by the late creator for the Youtube channel: The King of Random. Grant Thompson made the original design using a type of rubber/putty that is used to create the seal for the chamber. After the unfortunate passing of Grant, his mentors took over the channel and have been running the channel ever since. I will be using both Grant and NightHawkInLight's design for the vacuum chamber.

VR Headset

The VR headset is a DIY headset made for half the cost that can play many games that support Oculous, HTC Vive, or Sony headsets. The DIY headset will have 110 FOV with clear lenses and other displays on the outer body of the headset. The headset doesn't require and sensors to accurately show the user in any game. This design is made by Raspaholic Ind.

Haptic VR Gloves

Because of the cost of haptic VR gloves, I will be making my own using the design by Lucas VRTech. Lucas's design uses potentiometers and badge pulleys to map the distance of the users fingers contracting and the movement of the hand. These gloves will work hand in hand with the DIY VR headset and can play many games that hand movement like Half Life Alex. The gloves also have a joystick at the top of the assembly for movement of the user.

Starlite

Starlite is a material that's recipe was lost due to the creator having very strict rules over the material. Fortunately NightHawkInLight has made his own recipe that is being improved continuosly to better compare to the original material. Starlite is a material that acts as a heat shield on rockets. The material forms a wall of carbon when burned further pushing the heat source away from the material.
